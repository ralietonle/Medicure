Welcome to Medicure !


Medicure is a web application made by students of the National Advanced School of Engineering of Yaounde (NASEY). It allows you to monitor your vital parameters just using an Arduino board.

Get Started
Fast

With Medicure, you can monitor your parameters as fast as you want !

Secure

Send your parameters with the entire confidentiality. No one can spy on you!

Free

You can use Medicure as many times as you want. It is all free !
